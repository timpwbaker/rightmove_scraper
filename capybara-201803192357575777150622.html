Full description

Tenure: Freehold

Set on a quiet residential street in the Liverpool grove conservation area overlooking the popular Faraday gardens is this well-presented three bedroom Victorian house. Well-loved and looked after this family home boasts two reception rooms downstairs and a separate Kitchen, access to the low maintenance paved garden is provided by double doors from the dining room. On the first floor there are two double bedrooms, and a well-designed modern bathroom suite with stand-alone walk in shower, there is a further double bedroom in the converted loft complete with a velux window.

Date street is moments from Walworth road, with Elephant and Castle and Burgess park also within close proximity. Central London and the City are also at your fingertips with regular bus services from Walworth Road, and excellent train services from Kennington underground station and also Elephant and Castle (including the Overground service) Houses such as this do not come to the market often please register your interest early to avoid disappointment.
