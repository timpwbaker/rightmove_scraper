Full description

Tenure: Freehold

A four bedroom 1076sqft freehold house in this popular residential location on the eastern side of Thorburn Square conservation area. The house is in good order, and has numerous benefits, including off street parking and a decent size paved garden. The house has a separate kitchen/dining room, good size reception room and cloakroom on the ground floor, and four bedrooms and a bathroom upstairs. It's a superb family house in a great location with easy access to public transport and all amenities. This property is being sold with no onward chain.
